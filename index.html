Our story

We started Vita Nova after a thousand school runs went sideways.

Between us, we've spent over fifteen years inside UAE schools — running events, coordinating calendars, chasing suppliers, and standing next to nervous principals at 6am on event day. We loved the work and hated the chaos. The same last-minute rushes, the same vendor phone calls, the same frantic WhatsApp threads the night before.

So in 2024 we built the team we wished we'd had: one point of contact, transparent budgets, calm on the day, and a real plan B. Vita Nova is what event management looks like when it's run by people who've actually been on the other side of the clipboard.

Today we work with schools across all seven Emirates — and we still get to every load-in at sunrise.

How we work

A clear, calm process from kick-off to cleanup

  1. 01

    Discover

    We meet with your leadership team to understand goals, audience, tone, and constraints. We visit the venue and review the calendar.

  2. 02

    Design

    A creative brief, theme, run-of-show, and budget — presented for sign-off in plain language.

  3. 03

    Plan

    Suppliers, contracts, permits, risk assessments, comms, and a shared project timeline with weekly check-ins.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    On-site team from load-in to load-out. We run the rehearsal, the show, and the wrap.

  5. 05

    Review

    Photos, video, attendance and spend reports, plus a debrief so next year is even better.
